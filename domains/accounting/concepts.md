# Accounting Domain — Concept Dictionary and Interpretation Rules

## Double-Entry Bookkeeping Core Terms

- Debit (차변) = the side that records asset increases and expense occurrences. Also records decreases in liabilities/equity/revenue
- Credit (대변) = the side that records liability/equity increases and revenue occurrences. Also records decreases in assets/expenses
- Journal Entry (분개) = the act of decomposing a transaction into Debit (차변) and Credit (대변) entries for recording
- Posting (전기) = the procedure of transferring journal records to the General Ledger (총계정원장)
- Trial Balance (시산표) = a schedule verifying that the total of all account Debit (차변) balances equals the total of all Credit (대변) balances

## Financial Statement Terms

- Statement of Financial Position (재무상태표) = the state of assets, liabilities, and equity at a specific point in time. Former name: Balance Sheet (대차대조표)
- Statement of Comprehensive Income (포괄손익계산서) = revenue, expenses, and Other Comprehensive Income (기타포괄손익) for a given period. Former name: Income Statement (손익계산서)
- Statement of Cash Flows (현금흐름표) = cash inflows/outflows for a given period classified as operating/investing/financing
- Fair Value (공정가치) = the price receivable in an orderly transaction between market participants at the measurement date
- Carrying Amount (장부금액) = the amount at which an asset/liability is recognized on the Statement of Financial Position (재무상태표)

## Revenue Recognition Terms (IFRS 15)

- Performance Obligation (이행의무) = a promise to transfer a distinct good/service to the customer
- Transaction Price (거래가격) = the consideration expected upon completion of Performance Obligations (이행의무)
- Over Time (진행기준) = recognizing revenue progressively as Performance Obligations (이행의무) are fulfilled over a period
- At a Point in Time (시점기준) = recognizing revenue at the specific point when control transfers

## Financial Instruments Terms (IFRS 9)

- AC (Amortised Cost, 상각후원가) = recognizing interest income using the effective interest method while amortizing the principal
- FVOCI (Fair Value through OCI, 기타포괄손익-공정가치) = recognizing fair value changes in Other Comprehensive Income (기타포괄손익)
- FVTPL (Fair Value through P&L, 당기손익-공정가치) = recognizing fair value changes in profit or loss
- ECL (Expected Credit Loss, 기대신용손실) = loss estimates based on future expected default rates

## Lease Terms (IFRS 16)

- Right-of-Use Asset (사용권자산) = an asset representing the right to use an underlying asset for the lease term
- Lease Liability (리스부채) = the present value of the obligation to make lease payments
- Low-Value Lease (소액리스) = a lease where the underlying asset has a low value. Recognition exemption available
- Short-Term Lease (단기리스) = a lease with a term of 12 months or less. Recognition exemption available

## Tax Accounting Terms

- Tax Adjustment (세무조정) = the procedure of reconciling differences between K-IFRS income and taxable income
- Temporary Difference (일시적차이) = the difference between the Carrying Amount (장부금액) and tax base of assets/liabilities. The cause of Deferred Tax (이연법인세)
- Permanent Difference (영구적차이) = a difference between accounting and tax treatment that is never resolved. Does not generate Deferred Tax (이연법인세)

## Homonyms Requiring Attention

- "equity": Equity (자본, net assets on the Statement of Financial Position) ≠ Capital (자본, invested funds) ≠ Share Capital (자본금, proceeds from share issuance)
- "cost": Historical Cost (취득원가) ≠ Manufacturing Cost (제조원가) ≠ Opportunity Cost (기회원가)
- "loss": Net Loss (당기순손실) ≠ Impairment Loss (손상차손) ≠ Expected Credit Loss (기대신용손실)
- "value": Fair Value (공정가치) ≠ Carrying Amount (장부금액) ≠ Net Realizable Value (순실현가능가치, NRV) ≠ Value in Use (사용가치)
- "income": Operating Income (영업이익) ≠ Net Income (당기순이익) ≠ Other Comprehensive Income (기타포괄이익, OCI) ≠ EBITDA
- "recognition": Revenue Recognition (수익인식) ≠ Asset Recognition (자산인식) ≠ Provision Recognition (충당부채인식)

## Interpretation Principles

- The appropriateness of accounting treatment cannot be determined solely by formal compliance with standards. Under the substance over form principle, consistency between economic substance and form must be verified
- "Trial Balance (시산표) is in balance" and "accounting treatment is correct" are not the same. Debit (차변)/Credit (대변) balance does not guarantee classification accuracy
- The classification of accounts (assets/liabilities/revenue/expenses) is determined by the economic nature of the transaction. The same cash flow may be classified under different accounts depending on substance
- K-IFRS and tax standards have different purposes. Differences between accounting income and taxable income are not errors but arise from systemic differences
- Estimates (depreciation, allowance for doubtful accounts, etc.) involve judgment. The reasonableness of estimates is evaluated by whether the basis is explicitly stated
